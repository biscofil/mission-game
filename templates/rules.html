Rules

Here are the rules for the mission game:

  • The game requires at least 3 players to start.
  • The game lasts until the end of the night or for a predetermined amount of time.
  • Each player is assigned a secret mission and a target player.
  • All players play at the same time, trying to complete their missions at any time of the game.
  • Players can only see their own mission and the name of the player they are assigned to.
  • The game starts when all players are ready.
  • When a player successfully completes their mission with their target player, they will show their mission to the target player who should confirm the completion.
  • Not all players have to be informed of the elimination of a player. It can be kept secret between the player who eliminated and the eliminated player.
  • Upon completion of a mission, the player who completed it will eliminate their target player and take over their mission and target player.
  • The winner is the player who eliminates the most players by the end of the game.

Example

For example, if there are three players: Alice, Bob, and Carol.

  • Alice's mission: "Make Bob laugh."
  • Bob's mission: "Get Carol to sing a song."
  • Carol's mission: "Convince Alice to dance."

Alice must find a way to make Bob laugh without revealing her mission to anyone. Similarly, Bob and Carol must complete their missions without giving away their objectives.

If/when Alice succeeds in completing her mission, making Bob laugh, she will eliminate him from the game and she will get his mission and target player. Thus, she will have to get Carol to sing.

If no other elimination occurs until the end of the game, Alice will be the winner as she eliminated the most players (1) compared to Bob (0) and Carol (0).

Good luck and have fun!
